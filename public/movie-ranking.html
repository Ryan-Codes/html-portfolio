The Best Movies according to Ryan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Scott Pilgrim vs The World

This is a fantastic graphic novel put to feel that really keeps that graphic novel charm. It is a heartwarming story that also is not lacking in action.

Inception

One of the most genuinely creative ideas ever put to film.

Scream

This tongue-in-cheek slasher revitalized the slasher genre after it had grown stale in the 80's.
